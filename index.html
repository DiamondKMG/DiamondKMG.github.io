Welcome!

I am a consistently curious human who loves spending time outdoors and searching for answers to questions about the world around me. I'm most interested in why animals are shaped in such strange ways and how these shapes influence how animals move. To find out more about what I've worked on and what I'm currently working on check out my research page. If you have any questions that you think I could help answer please reach out! From debating hypothetical animal matchups to figuing out how to use techniques from math and physics to quantify animal shape and motions, I love talking all kinds of science!
